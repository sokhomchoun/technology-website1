Qualcomm has announced that it will officially unveil a new chip that supports AI functionality for Microsoft Windows PCs, and claims that it will be faster than Apple's chip. On another Mac.

Those AI features will also be a key part of Qualcomm's chips for smartphones, as Google and Meta have both expressed satisfaction and plans to use them. ... At the same time, Microsoft called the new chip will help businesses and consumers in the new era of AI PCs.

According to Qualcomm on October 24, the new chip is faster than the M2 Max in some functions and more powerful than Apple and Intel chips.
